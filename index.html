About me

"A volcano hidden behind a flower". That's how a professor in middle school described me: cold, calm and collected from the outside, but passionate and curious on the inside. I think this is the perfect sentence to describe me. I've always been a bit of a nerd and when I started programming in high school I found my affinity with coding. But I've always found the way coding is taught in school very unattractive and outdated and so I resorted to online learning through platforms like Scrimba.com and freecodecamp.org. Learning new technologies is what reignited my passion for coding.
